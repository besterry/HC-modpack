Recycle Plastic Bags
By MrRAT, 2019.
Downloaded from Steam Workshop.

This mod adds recipie to recycle plastic bags into garbage bags.

Do you find Plastic Bags useless, wouldn't it be great to to be able to make Rain 
Collector Barrels from them? For ease of use, this mods don't interfere with Carpenty 
recipies, but adds simple recipy to make Garbage Bag from Plastic Bag using scissors.
